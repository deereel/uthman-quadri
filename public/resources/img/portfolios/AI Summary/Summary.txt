Short Summary

This automation streamlines your client onboarding by turning every Google Form submission into a clean, ready-to-use CRM record. When someone submits your form, their message is automatically summarized using Google Gemini AI, highlighting key details like project type, goals, timeline, and budget. The system then checks your Airtable CRM for existing leads and either updates or creates the record instantly.
