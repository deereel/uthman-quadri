Short Summary

The Crypto Watcher system monitors selected cryptocurrencies, fetches real-time market and trending data, sends alerts through Telegram and Gmail, stores all coin data in Supabase, and displays it on a Lovable-built dashboard—creating a full automation pipeline from tracking to notification to UI.


Tools 

Google Sheets - Make.com – Crypto API - Telegram Bot – Gmail – Supabase – Lovable (UI App Builder)
